Managing LOB Attributes

This facility enables you to add, edit, and delete a LOB Attributes.

How to add a LOB Attributes?

1. On the menu toolbar, go to "Business Permit -> Master -> LOB Attributes" item.



2. Click on the "New" button.
3. And fill in the Name, Description fields.



4. Click on the "Save" button.





How to edit a LOB Attributes?

1. Go to "LOB Attributes" page. (Please refer to the Added LOB Attributes - Step 1)
2. Select an item from the lob attributes list, and click on the "Edit" button.
3. Modify the selected LOB Attributes item.



4. Then click "Save" button.




How to delete a LOB Attributes?

1. Go to "LOB Attributes" page. (Please refer to the Added LOB Attributes - Step 1)
2. Select an item from the lob attributes list, and click on the "Delete" button.



4. Then click "Save" button.
