Managing Line of Business

This facility enables you to add, edit, and delete a Line of Business.

How to add a Line of Business?

1. On the menu toolbar, go to "Business Permit -> Master -> Line of Business" item.



2. Click on the "New" button.
3. And fill in the Line of Business, Classification and Attribute fields.



Note: Classification and Attributes are lookup fields.




LOB Classification lookup



LOB Attribute lookup




4. Click on the "Save" button.





4. Then Click on the "Approve" button.





How to edit a Line of Business?

1. Go to "Line of Business" page. (Please refer to the Added Line of Business - Step 1)
2. Select an item from the line of business list, and click on the "Edit" button.
3. Modify the selected Line of Business item.



4. Then click "Save" button.




How to delete a Line of Business?

1. Go to "Line of Business" page. (Please refer to the Added Line of Business - Step 1)
2. Select an item from the line of business list, and click on the "Delete" button.
