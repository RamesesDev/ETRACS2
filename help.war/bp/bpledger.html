How to process a Business Ledger?

On the Home page, click on the Business Ledger icon.



1. Lookup the Trade name in the Trade Name field.
Note: Just search the keyword for the trade name or the taxpayer name.



Select an item then OK.

Business Lookup



3. Switch in to Payments tab then click Capture Payment button.



4. Fill in all the required fields then click Next.



Note: You can modify the Amount Paid Column
if there's a descrepancy in your previous application assesment.

5. Then Post.



Note: Make sure that document that you are about to post
is correct or accurate base into your reference document (OR).
