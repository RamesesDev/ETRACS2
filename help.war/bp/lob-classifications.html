Managing LOB Classifications

This facility enables you to add, edit, and delete a LOB Classifications.

How to add a LOB Classifications?

1. On the menu toolbar, go to "Business Permit -> Master -> LOB Classifications" item.



2. Click on the "New" button.
3. and Fill in the Classification, Remarks fields.



4. Click on the "Save" button.





How to edit a LOB Classifications?

1. Go to "LOB Classifications" page. (Please refer to the Added LOB Classifications - Step 1)
2. Select an item from the lob classification list, and click on the "Edit" button.
3. Modify the selected LOB Classification item.



4. then click "Save" button.




How to delete a LOB Classifications?

1. Go to "LOB Classifications" page. (Please refer to the Added LOB Classifications - Step 1)
2. Select an item from the lob classification list, and click on the "Delete" button.



4. then click "Save" button.
