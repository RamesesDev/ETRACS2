How to process a Renew Business (Capture) Applications?

On the Home page, click on the Business Transaction icon.



1. Select an item to be process and click on the Process button.



2. Lookup the Tax Payer in the Application No. field.
Note: Just search the keyword for the taxpayer or the application no.



Entity Lookup



3. Fill in all the required fields and Save.



4. Review the filled in data and then Submit.



5. Assess the application.



6. Fill in all the required fields then click Next.



7. Submit the application for approval.



8. And Approve.
